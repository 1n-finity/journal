About the Journal

Vivtih Journal is an open access, peer-reviewed journal for research in food science and clinical nutrition. We welcome quality papers from researchers worldwide in areas including nutritional biochemistry, food technology, clinical nutrition, public health nutrition, and related fields. We also publish reviews, editorials, and invited commentary.
