Author's Instructions

Authorship and Contributions

Authors must meet the following criteria: • Contributed substantially to the represented work in terms of conceptual design or analysis, writing of the article and final approval. • Accountability for all aspects of the work, ensuring integrity and accuracy.

Author’s Responsibilities

Authors are responsible for ensuring that the content they submit does not infringe upon any third-party intellectual property rights, including copyright, trademarks, and patents. Authors must verify that they have the necessary permissions for all copyrighted materials included in their submission. Manuscripts requiring revision should address all reviewer comments comprehensively.

Plagiarism Policy

We are committed to publishing original work and use plagiarism detection software to screen all submissions. Manuscripts with significant overlap will be rejected.

Author Guidelines

All manuscripts must be submitted online through the website [to be created]. Authors do not have to pay for the submission of articles. Submissions not following instructions will be returned for technical correction. Manuscripts must be in doc/docx format.

Cover Letter Guidelines

The cover letter must include: • Manuscript Type and Title • Research Area • Authorship Declaration • Copyright Transfer Statement • Corresponding Author Details

Manuscript Types

Original Articles: up to 6,000 words, max 50 references, 8 tables/figures. Review Articles: up to 8,000 words, max 100 references, 8 tables/figures. Mini Reviews: 2,000–3,000 words, max 40 references, 4 tables/figures. Short Reports: up to 3,000 words, max 25 references, 3 tables/figures. Case Studies: 1,500–2,500 words. Protocol Papers: 3,000–4,000 words. White Papers: 3,000–5,000 words. Policy Briefs: 1,500–2,000 words. Educational Resources: 2,000–3,000 words. Perspectives: 2,000–2,500 words. Opinion Papers: 1,000–1,500 words. Commentaries: ≤1,500 words. Editorials: ≤1,200 words. Letters to the Editor: ≤800 words. Selected Summaries: ≤1,000 words.

Manuscript Preparation

Formatting, Title Page, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Acknowledgements, Funding, Conflict of Interest, Data Availability, Author Contributions.

Use of AI in Manuscripts

Generative AI tools cannot be listed as authors. Any use must be disclosed in Acknowledgements or Methods.

Supplementary Files

Authors are encouraged to provide supplementary materials that enhance transparency. Files should be in open-access repositories.

References

Must conform to Vancouver style, numbered consecutively in-text.

Tables & Figures

Tables must be self-explanatory; figures in JPEG/PNG ≥300 dpi; legends concise ≤40 words.

Protection of Patients’ Rights

Identifiable information must not be published without consent. Consent forms not submitted to the journal.

Revised Manuscripts

Include referees’ remarks and mark changes with underline or color.

Checklist

Cover letter signed, funding, conflicts disclosed, author details, formatting, tables & figures compliance.

Regular APC

Discounts & Waivers

  • 20% discount for students & first-year PhD researchers
  • 30% waiver for editors (max 2 manuscripts/year)
  • 20% waiver per review completed (max 2/year)

APC is payable only after acceptance. Withdrawal within 6 days is free; after review/acceptance, 50% withdrawal fee applies.
