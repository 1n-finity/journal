Editorial Policy

Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is dedicated to ensuring the integrity, quality, and accessibility of the scientific content we publish. This policy serves as a comprehensive guide for authors, reviewers, editors, and readers regarding the journal's editorial standards and practices.

We follow the ethical principles outlined by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) and maintain transparency at every stage of the editorial and publication process.

Scope and Objectives

The journal focuses on advancing research in the fields of clinical nutrition, food science, and food technology disciplines.

  • Promote the dissemination of innovative research
  • Provide a platform for global collaboration and knowledge exchange
  • Enhance awareness and understanding of critical topics in nutrition and food science
Peer Review Policy

Our journal employs a double-blind peer review process to ensure fairness and objectivity. All submitted manuscripts are initially reviewed by editors for suitability for formal review.

Review Process

  1. Manuscript Submission – Authors submit via online portal or email
  2. Initial Screening – Check for plagiarism and ethical violations
  3. Assignment to Editors – Assign to relevant Associate Editor
  4. Peer Review – Two qualified reviewers evaluate the manuscript
  5. Editorial Decision – Final decision based on reviewer feedback
  6. Publication – Copyediting and online publication

Decision Criteria

  • Relevance to journal scope
  • Originality and contribution to knowledge
  • Scientific rigor and methodology
  • Clarity of writing and organization
  • Ethical integrity

Editorial Responsibilities

Editor in Chief

  • Oversees editorial strategy and quality
  • Manages complex cases and ethical issues
  • Final decision authority

Associate Editors

  • Manage peer review process
  • Select appropriate reviewers
  • Make editorial decisions

Managing Editors

  • Handle administrative tasks
  • Assign manuscripts to editors
  • Ensure timely processing
