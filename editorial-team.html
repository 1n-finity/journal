Editorial Board

Editors-in-Chief

MaryCatherine Bridges

Co-Editor-in-Chief

University of Dallas

MaryCatherine Bridges is a faculty member in the Department of Nutritional Sciences at the University of Dallas. Her research interests include clinical nutrition, particularly micronutrient deficiencies and their therapeutic interventions. She has published extensively on vitamin D metabolism and its role in chronic disease prevention.

mbridges@Vivrtihjournal.com

Emilio Montalvo

Co-Editor-in-Chief

University of Dallas

Dr. Emilio Montalvo specializes in food science methodology and the intersection of nutrition with biochemistry. His work focuses on the development of nutritional research methods in the 21st century and the role of molecular analysis in nutrition science.

emontalvo@Vivrtihjournal.com

Becoming a Reviewer

We welcome applications from qualified scholars to join our pool of peer reviewers. Reviewers should have:

  • Advanced degree (Ph.D. or equivalent) in Economics, History, or related field
  • Publications in food science, clinical nutrition, or related areas
  • Experience in academic review processes
  • Commitment to timely and constructive reviews

Interested scholars should contact our editorial team with their CV and areas of expertise.
